## 🔬 Current Research — VATSA

**VATSA (Video · Audio · Text · Sensory · Action)** is a unified five-modality AI architecture for human-level perception and action. Each modality encoder projects into a shared 512-dimensional latent space for cross-modal fusion.

- ✅ **V-Module complete** — EfficientNet-B0: 96.31% on CIFAR-10 · YOLOv8: 22 FPS · 1,336 embeddings/sec
- 🔄 **Audio, Text, Sensory, Action modules** — in progress (DBA research timeline 2025–2028)
- 📄 **Preprint published** — Zenodo, April 2026

**SAMOS (Safety-Aware Multi-Output Selector)** — proposed novel output routing mechanism using asymmetric safety-weighted sigmoid thresholds per modality. Enables safe parallel multi-modal output generation in physically embodied AI systems. The ethical principle — *a robot must never harm a human, even accidentally, even when uncertain* — encoded directly into the architecture.
